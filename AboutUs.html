Welcome to Journey of Faith: Exploring the Stations of the Cross in Manduyog Hill, a virtual pilgrimage experience crafted to guide you through one of the Philippines' most cherished spiritual sites—Manduyog Hill in Banga, Aklan. Our mission is to offer a meaningful way for visitors, devotees, and the faithful to embark on a reflective journey, wherever they may be.

Manduyog Hill has long been a place of reverence, drawing pilgrims and travelers to its sacred grounds, where each of the 14 Stations of the Cross invites personal reflection, prayer, and renewal. Through our immersive virtual tour, we bring this holy place to life with detailed visuals, inspiring narratives, and a serene atmosphere designed to guide you through every step of the Passion of Christ.

Our team combines faith-driven storytelling, high-quality imagery, and local insights to create a virtual experience that is as close to the physical pilgrimage as possible. Whether you’re seeking to deepen your faith, explore a unique cultural landmark, or prepare for an in-person visit, Journey of Faith is here to help you connect spiritually to Manduyog Hill's Stations of the Cross.

Join us in this sacred journey and find inspiration, peace, and devotion at every station.
